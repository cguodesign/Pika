INTRODUCTION

The share economy has blossomed with the proliferation of mobile computing, and we are no exception. Our customers are everyday people that need an item delivered, but lack the means or motivation to do it themselves. The customer does not necessarily need to possess the item at the time of the delivery/pickup request; they simply need to specify an origin, a destination, and a time they would like their item delivered. We believe this is a convenience that many people would pay for and that the product we are developing can unlock the potential of the Mobile Delivery Market.
